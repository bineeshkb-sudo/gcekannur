Home/Civil Engineering

Structures, geotechnics, transportation, and water systems

One of GCEK's founding departments, with strong academic depth in core civil engineering and postgraduate research.

The Civil Engineering department has been functioning at GCEK since 1986. The current college site highlights its long-standing role in teaching, research, consultancy, publications, workshops, site visits, and student-centred professional activities.

Department Snapshot

1986 origin, UG intake of 60, two PG programmes, and research-centre status

The official department page notes that Civil Engineering has operated since 1986, offers B.Tech with an intake of 60, and runs M.Tech programmes in Computer Aided Structural Engineering and Geotechnical and Geo-Environmental Engineering with an intake of 18 each. The college accreditation page lists the UG programme and M.Tech Computer Aided Structural Engineering under accreditation for 2023-2026.

Overview

Department profile based on the current GCEK site

Academic Identity

The live department page describes Civil Engineering as one of the early GCEK departments, with graduates contributing to infrastructure planning and execution in India and abroad. The department positions itself around quality education, sustainable built-environment thinking, and strong professional grounding.

Research and Consultancy

The official profile says the department was recognized as a research centre under Kannur University in 2006 and has built its academic presence through consultancy, collaborative work, publications, conferences, FDPs, and technical programmes.

Student Exposure

The current site emphasizes site visits, project tours, workshops, and student programmes as part of the department culture, alongside strong laboratory support in surveying, structural analysis, geotechnics, environmental engineering, and transportation.

Programmes

Current programmes highlighted for Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department currently presents three major academic offerings in the public profile: B.Tech Civil Engineering, M.Tech Computer Aided Structural Engineering, and M.Tech Geotechnical and Geo-Environmental Engineering. The official accreditation page lists the UG Civil Engineering programme and the M.Tech Computer Aided Structural Engineering programme under accreditation for the 2023-2026 cycle.

Laboratories

Facilities listed in the official department pages

Surveying and CSA Facilities

The live department pages list Surveying Lab and CSA Lab among the core facilities that support field measurement, mapping, and analysis.

Geotechnical and Environmental Labs

Geotechnical Engineering Lab and Environmental Engineering Lab support soil, foundation, and environmental testing requirements across UG, PG, and project work.

Transportation and Materials Testing

The department also lists Transportation Engineering Lab and Strength of Materials / Concrete Testing Lab for pavement, traffic, concrete, and strength-related practical work.

Advanced Design Support

Non-Destructive Testing Lab, Project Room, and CAD / Structural Engineering Design Studio Lab extend the department's project, consultancy, and design capability.
