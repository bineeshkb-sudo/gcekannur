Career Support

What the CGPU does for students

According to the live CGPU pages, the placement unit guides students toward placements in credible organizations, conducts placement drives, and organizes training, seminars, group discussions, and mock interviews as part of career guidance and personality development.

The same pages also say the unit coordinates student efforts for industrial training, serves as an information centre for opportunities and competitive examinations such as GATE, GRE, and CAT, and acts as a link between alumni and students.
