Message

Principal's welcome note

The current message emphasizes that GCEK serves students through a combination of established academic programmes, research activity, placement support, co-curricular forums, and a campus environment designed to support both professional growth and personal development.

It points to undergraduate programmes in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Communication, and Computer Science and Engineering, along with postgraduate and doctoral opportunities. The message also highlights the institution's research culture, Centre of Excellence status in Systems, Energy and Environment, and collaborations that strengthen teaching and research.

The principal's message further notes the contribution of hostels, staff quarters, PTA involvement, training and placement support, student forums, alumni activity, and industrial linkages in shaping students into capable professionals with practical skills and entrepreneurial potential.

Priorities

Institutional priorities reflected in the current message

The live message places strong emphasis on quality technical education, research engagement, placement and counselling support, co-curricular growth, and effective use of industry connections. It presents GCEK as an institution where faculty, staff, and facilities work together to help students develop both professionally and individually.

The current site also includes a roll of principals, reflecting the institutional continuity and leadership history of the college from its founding years to recent administrations.
