Admission Routes

Programme-wise entry pathways

Regular UG Admission

The live page states that candidates who pass the pre-degree examination of Kerala universities, or an equivalent examination, with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as optional subjects are eligible subject to required marks.

Minimum Marks

The current requirement mentions a minimum of 50% marks in Mathematics and 50% marks in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry taken together for general eligibility.

Lateral Entry

The current site also notes eligibility for diploma holders in engineering who have completed an approved institutional course of at least three years and secured the prescribed minimum in the final diploma examination.

Eligibility

Category-based admission conditions

The present admission page says that socially and educationally backward classes of Kerala, as approved by Government, with total family income below the prescribed threshold are eligible with a reduced minimum mark requirement. For diploma-based lateral entry, the live page similarly notes a reduced minimum for eligible SEBC applicants under the stated income limit.

This redesigned page can later be extended with the exact current admission procedure, allotment rounds, fee payment, and reporting instructions when those details are finalized for a specific cycle.
