Design, manufacturing, thermal systems, and applied mechanics

A founding department with strong workshop culture, postgraduate depth, and broad laboratory support.

The official Mechanical Engineering pages describe the department as functioning since the inception of the college in 1986, with strong student outcomes in placements, higher studies, and professionally oriented learning.

Department Snapshot

1986 origin, UG intake growth, postgraduate programmes, and accredited UG status

The live department site says Mechanical Engineering started in 1986 with 40 students in the UG programme, increased intake to 60 in 1999, and offers postgraduate work in Advanced Manufacturing and Mechanical Systems Design. The college PG programme page also lists Energy Engineering under Mechanical Engineering, and the accreditation page lists the UG programme in the 2023-2026 cycle.

Overview

Department profile based on the current GCEK site

Academic Identity

The public Mechanical Engineering profile says the department has functioned since 1986 and has grown from an initial intake of 40 to 60 in the undergraduate programme. It presents itself as a strong destination for excellence in contemporary mechanical engineering education.

Programmes and Scale

The official site notes the presence of postgraduate study in Advanced Manufacturing and Mechanical Systems Design, while the college PG list also includes Energy Engineering under Mechanical Engineering. The same page states a total student strength of 301 and sanctioned support for faculty and staff.

Student Development

The current department profile highlights strong grades, campus recruitment in reputed companies, and admissions to higher studies in leading institutes in India and abroad.

Programmes

Current programmes highlighted for Mechanical Engineering

The public GCEK programme pages present B.Tech Mechanical Engineering as the core undergraduate offering. They also list M.Tech Advanced Manufacturing and Mechanical Systems Design and M.Tech Energy Engineering under the department. The college accreditation page lists the UG Mechanical Engineering programme in the 2023-2026 accreditation cycle.

Laboratories

Facilities listed in the official department pages

Workshop and Manufacturing Facilities

The department site lists the Mechanical Engineering Workshop, Production Engineering Lab, Advanced Manufacturing Lab, and CAD / Computational Lab as major facilities for design and fabrication support.

Thermal and Energy Labs

Thermal Engineering Lab, Heat Transfer Lab, and Energy Engineering Lab support thermal systems, heat-flow understanding, and applied energy studies.

Fluids and Dynamics

Fluid Mechanics and Fluid Machinery Lab together with the Dynamics Lab support experimentation in motion, machines, and fluid-system analysis.

Instrumentation and Project Work

The Instrumentation Lab, Engineering Software Lab, Project Lab, Seminar Hall, and Department Library help extend the department's practical and academic ecosystem.
